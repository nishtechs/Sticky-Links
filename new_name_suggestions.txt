Hello! Of course, I'd be happy to help you brainstorm some new names for your app.

Since Sticky Links focuses on organization,
preservation, and a premium Flutter experience,
here are a few suggestions categorized by the "vibe" they project:


🌟 Premium & Professional (Focus on reliability and quality)
LinkVault – Emphasizes that your links are safe and preserved.
Nexus Link – Sounds central, connected, and high-tech.
Anchor – Suggests that these are the important links you "anchor" yourself to.
Portal – A sleek way to describe a gateway to your saved web content.
OmniList – Suggests an all-encompassing, powerful organization tool.

⚡ Minimalist & Punchy (Short, memorable, and modern)
Lync – A stylized play on "Link" and "Sync."
Tack – Keeping the "sticky/pinned" metaphor but making it shorter.
Stash – Simple and suggests a personal collection.
Klip – Modern, tech-focused spelling of "Clip."
Node – Perfect if you want to lean into the idea of a personal knowledge network.

📂 Organization & Productivity (Focus on the workflow)
LinkShelf – Makes the app feel like a library for your URLs.
WebNest – Gives a cozy, organized feeling of "collecting" bits of the web.
FlowLinks – Suggests a smooth, productive navigation experience.
Index – Clean, classic, and implies perfect categorization.
Curate – Highlights the "Smart Categorization" feature of the app.

🎨 Creative & Modern
Glance – Suggests how quickly you can find what you need.
Orbit – Implies that your most important links are always revolving around you.
Prism – Sounds premium and relates to the different "colors/themes" in your UI.
Paperclip – A nostalgic but clean reference to holding things together.
My personal favorite? I think LinkVault or Lync would fit the 
"premium" and "modern" description in your README perfectly!
